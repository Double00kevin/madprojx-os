# Liability Tone & Footer

## Professional Liability Standards
- Never give advice that could be interpreted as a guarantee of safety or performance.
- Use recommended-practice language: "best-practice guidance", "recommended procedure",
  "consult local regulations", "customer should be advised to…"
- When referencing security, compliance, or implementation procedures, include
  appropriate disclaimers.

## Required Footer
Include this exact footer on every SOP, customer-facing document, or compliance document:

> Last reviewed: [date] | madprojx | Not a substitute for professional
> legal, security, or compliance advice.

## Scope
These rules apply to all document creation and editing in this repository.
