# madprojx — Service Offerings

## Service Funnel

Assessment → Sprint → Advisory. Each stage is independently valuable and leads naturally to the next.

---

## 1. AI Readiness Assessment

**Type:** Productized service — fixed scope, fixed price
**Duration:** 2 weeks
**Pricing:** TBD (placeholder: $1,500–$3,000 depending on business size)

**What the client gets:**
- 1-hour discovery call to understand their business, workflows, and pain points
- Review of current tools, processes, and data handling practices
- Identification of top 3 AI opportunities ranked by impact and feasibility
- Security and privacy risk assessment for each opportunity
- Written deliverable: AI Readiness Report with prioritized recommendations, estimated costs, and implementation timeline
- 30-minute follow-up call to walk through the report

**What we evaluate:**
- Where is time being wasted on repetitive tasks?
- Where is data being handled manually that could be automated?
- What tools are they already paying for that have AI features they're not using?
- What are the security/privacy risks of their current setup?
- What's realistic given their budget and technical capacity?

**Ideal client:** Any small business that's curious about AI but hasn't done anything yet. This is the entry point.

**Delivery:** Remote. Report delivered as PDF. Follow-up via video call.

## 2. AI Implementation Sprint

**Type:** Project-based engagement — scoped, delivered, done
**Duration:** 2–6 weeks depending on complexity
**Pricing:** TBD (placeholder: $3,000–$10,000 depending on scope)

**What the client gets:**
- Implementation of the top recommendation from the Assessment (or a specific AI initiative they've identified)
- Tool selection, configuration, and integration into their existing workflow
- Security review of the chosen solution (data handling, access controls, vendor terms)
- Staff training on how to use the new tool effectively and safely
- Written deliverable: Implementation Summary with configuration details, security notes, and maintenance instructions
- 2 weeks of post-implementation support via email

**Example projects:**
- Setting up an AI-powered customer service chatbot for their website
- Automating invoice processing and data entry
- Implementing AI-assisted email triage and response drafting
- Configuring AI-powered inventory forecasting
- Building automated report generation from their existing data

**Ideal client:** A business that completed an Assessment or already knows what they want to implement and needs someone to do it right.

---

## 3. Ongoing AI Advisory

**Type:** Monthly retainer
**Duration:** Ongoing, month-to-month
**Pricing:** TBD (placeholder: $500–$1,500/month)

**What the client gets:**
- Monthly check-in call (30–60 minutes) to review AI usage, identify new opportunities, and address issues
- Priority email support for AI-related questions
- Quarterly security review of their AI tools and data practices
- Recommendations as new tools and capabilities become available
- First look at any new AI risks or vulnerabilities relevant to their setup

**What this is NOT:**
- Not a help desk or IT support contract
- Not unlimited consulting hours
- Not a commitment to implement — implementation is a separate Sprint engagement

**Ideal client:** A business that completed an Assessment or Sprint and wants ongoing guidance as AI evolves. This is the relationship that compounds over time.

## Pricing Notes

- All pricing is placeholder until market validation
- The Assessment is the lead product — price it to be a no-brainer for a small business owner
- Sprint pricing should be scoped per project, not hourly
- Advisory retainer should feel like "cheap insurance" compared to making AI mistakes alone
- Consider offering Assessment credit toward a Sprint (e.g., "your $2,000 Assessment fee is applied to any Sprint engagement within 90 days")
- Revisit pricing quarterly based on demand and delivery time

## What We Don't Offer

- Hourly consulting (scoped engagements only)
- AI model training or custom ML development
- Enterprise rollouts (50+ users)
- Ongoing IT support or managed services
- Guaranteed ROI claims
