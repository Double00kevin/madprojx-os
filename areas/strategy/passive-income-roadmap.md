# madprojx — Passive Income Roadmap

## The Two-Layer Model

### Layer 1: Consulting (Active Income + Credibility Engine)
AI Readiness Assessments, Implementation Sprints, Advisory retainers. Every client engagement generates real-world stories, lessons, and proof points that feed Layer 2. This is the foundation — real revenue, real credibility.

### Layer 2: Thought Leadership (Audience → Passive Income)
Kevin becomes the "security-first AI guy for small business." Content builds audience, audience unlocks monetization channels over time. The consulting work provides authentic material that no pure content creator can match.

## Content Production Model

**Daily input (Kevin):** Voice memos, observations, client war stories (anonymized), hot takes on AI news, mistakes observed in the wild. Raw, unpolished, authentic.

**AI production engine (KITT — Month 2-3 build):** Takes raw inputs and produces platform-specific content. Voice memo → transcription (Whisper) → content generation pipeline → drafts to review queue → Kevin approves/edits → publish.

**One input, multiple outputs:** A single 5-minute voice memo becomes a LinkedIn post, a Twitter/X thread, a blog post section, a newsletter segment, and a short-form video script.

## How the Layers Reinforce Each Other

- Consulting generates authentic content (real problems, real solutions)
- Content builds audience and credibility
- Audience generates inbound consulting leads (reducing outreach burden)
- Consulting clients see the content and trust Kevin before the first call
- Digital products are built from frameworks developed during consulting engagements
- Every engagement letter, workflow, and checklist becomes a potential template product

## Revenue Goal Structure

- **Layer 1 (Consulting):** Active income. Goal is to sustain operations and fund Layer 2 build-out.
- **Layer 2 (Content/Products):** Passive/semi-passive income. Goal is recurring revenue that doesn't require Kevin's direct time per transaction.
- **Combined target:** Layer 2 eventually matches or exceeds Layer 1, creating optionality.

## What We're NOT Doing

- No auto-posting bot farms. Every piece of content is Kevin-approved.
- No fake engagement or growth hacking tactics.
- No content that doesn't connect back to real expertise and real experience.
- No paid ads for audience building (organic only until product-market fit is proven).
