# LinkedIn Post 3 — "You're already paying for AI"

**Pillar:** AI Made Simple
**Status:** Draft
**Target publish:** 2026-03-17

---

Most small businesses are already paying for AI.

They just don't know it.

Microsoft 365 has Copilot features built in. Google Workspace has Gemini. QuickBooks, Salesforce, HubSpot, even Canva — they've all added AI in the last year.

But most small businesses are still paying the same subscription price and using zero of those features.

Before you go shopping for a new AI tool, check what you already own.

Open your most-used software. Look for anything labeled "AI," "smart," "auto," or "suggested." Chances are there's a feature sitting there that could save your team a few hours a week — and you've been paying for it since the last update.

The cheapest AI tool is the one you already have.

What software does your business use every day? I'll tell you what AI features are hiding inside it.
