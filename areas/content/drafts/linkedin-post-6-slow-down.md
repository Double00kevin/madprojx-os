# LinkedIn Post 6 — "Slow down on AI"

**Pillar:** Personal/Hot Take
**Status:** Draft
**Target publish:** 2026-03-20

---

Unpopular opinion: most small businesses should slow down on AI.

Not stop. Slow down.

I see the posts every day — "10 AI tools that will transform your business." "If you're not using AI, you're already behind." "AI will replace your competitors before it replaces you."

It's noise. And it's causing small business owners to rush into tools they don't understand, with data they can't get back, solving problems they haven't clearly defined.

I spent 20 years in cybersecurity watching companies rush to adopt new technology because they were afraid of being left behind. Most of the expensive mistakes I cleaned up weren't caused by bad technology. They were caused by speed.

AI is powerful. AI is real. And AI is not going anywhere.

But you have time to do this right.

Pick one problem. Understand the tool. Check the terms. Train your team. Then expand.

The businesses that win with AI won't be the ones that moved fastest. They'll be the ones that moved smart.

What's your biggest hesitation about AI right now?
