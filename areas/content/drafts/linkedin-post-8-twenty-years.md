# LinkedIn Post 8 — "Twenty years of protecting businesses"

**Pillar:** Story/Reflection
**Status:** Draft
**Target publish:** 2026-03-22

---

I spent 20 years protecting businesses from threats they couldn't see.

Misconfigured firewalls. Unpatched servers. Employees clicking links they shouldn't. Vendors with access they shouldn't have.

The pattern was always the same: a new technology shows up, everyone rushes to adopt it, and security is an afterthought. Then the breach happens, and suddenly security is the only thought.

I'm watching the exact same pattern with AI.

Small businesses are adopting AI tools faster than any technology I've seen in two decades. And almost none of them are asking the security questions first.

That's why I started madprojx. Not because AI is dangerous — it's not. It's incredibly useful. But useful and safe aren't the same thing.

My job is to make sure they are.

If you're a small business owner trying to figure out AI — you're not behind. You're right on time. The ones asking questions before jumping in are the ones who won't be cleaning up messes later.
