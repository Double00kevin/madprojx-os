# LinkedIn Post 4 — "Start with the time"

**Pillar:** Practical AI Use Cases
**Status:** Draft
**Target publish:** 2026-03-18

---

A 12-person accounting firm was spending 8 hours every week on the same task.

Pull data from client documents. Type it into their system. Repeat.

Every week. Same process, different documents.

They set up one AI tool — $50 a month — that reads the documents and pulls the data automatically. Their team reviews the output instead of doing the entry.

8 hours became 45 minutes.

They didn't need a consultant to tell them AI is important. They needed someone to look at their week and say "that task right there — AI can handle it."

If you're wondering where to start with AI, don't start with the technology.

Start with the time.

Find the task your team does every week that never changes. The one that eats hours and nobody enjoys.

That's your first AI project.

Where does your team lose the most time every week?
